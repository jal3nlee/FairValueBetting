How it works

A disciplined workflow: screen books, remove the vig, quantify edge, and size with Kelly.

Step 1

Screen books

Aggregate lines across sportsbooks and identify the best available price.

Step 2

De-vig odds

Remove the bookmaker’s margin to estimate fair win probabilities.

Step 3

Quantify edge

Compute market-implied EV% versus the top available number.

Step 4

Size with Kelly

Suggest stake sizes based on bankroll and your chosen Kelly factor.

Step 5

Filter & act

Rank by EV%, set thresholds, and place disciplined, repeatable bets.
