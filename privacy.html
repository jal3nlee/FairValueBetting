Privacy

Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy. This page explains what we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Last updated: Aug 2025

At a glance

  • We collect account info you provide and usage data to improve the product.
  • We use cookies/analytics for performance and basic personalization.
  • We don’t sell your personal information.
  • You can request access, correction, or deletion at any time.

Information we collect

  • Account & profile: name, email, and any details you add (e.g., preferences).
  • App usage: pages viewed, features used, interactions, timestamps, and referring pages.
  • Device & technical: IP address, browser type, device identifiers, approximate location (derived from IP).
  • Cookies & similar tech: to keep you signed in, remember settings, and measure performance.
  • Payments: if you subscribe, payments are processed by a third-party provider; we receive limited details (e.g., status, plan) but not full card numbers.
  • Communications: messages you send us (support, feedback, surveys).

How we use information

  • Provide, secure, and maintain the service (authentication, fraud prevention, debugging).
  • Improve features and performance (analytics, testing, troubleshooting).
  • Personalize your experience (saved settings, reminders, preferences).
  • Communicate with you (account notices, service updates, marketing with your consent).
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms.

Legal bases (where applicable)

Depending on your location, we process data under legal bases such as performance of a contract (to provide the service), legitimate interests (e.g., to improve and secure the product), consent (e.g., marketing), and compliance with legal obligations.

Sharing your information

  • Service providers: hosting, analytics, customer support, payment processing.
  • Legal & safety: to comply with law or protect rights, safety, and security.
  • Business transfers: if we explore or complete a merger, acquisition, or asset sale.

We do not sell your personal information.

Data retention

We keep information for as long as needed to provide the service and for legitimate business or legal purposes. We delete or anonymize data when it’s no longer required.

Security

We use administrative, technical, and organizational measures designed to protect your information. No online service can be 100% secure, so please use strong passwords and keep your account details safe.

Your choices & rights

  • Access/Update: update profile details in your account or contact us.
  • Delete: request deletion of your account and associated personal data.
  • Opt out of marketing: use unsubscribe links in emails or contact us.
  • Cookies: adjust browser settings to refuse or limit cookies (may affect functionality).
  • Regional rights: depending on your jurisdiction, you may have additional rights (e.g., portability, objection). Contact us to exercise them.

International users

We may process and store information in the United States and other countries. By using the service, you acknowledge that your data may be transferred to jurisdictions with different data protection laws.

Children

Our service is not intended for individuals under the legal betting age in their jurisdiction (often 21+ in the U.S.). We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you believe a minor has provided data, contact us and we will take appropriate steps.

Changes & contact

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we’ll post a notice on this page or within the app. Your continued use of the service after changes means you accept the updated policy.

Questions or requests? Email us at hello@fairvaluebetting.com.
