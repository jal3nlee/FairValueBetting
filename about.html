About Fair Value Sports

Why Fair Value Sports

We convert market prices into fair probabilities, quantify edge, and size positions—so you can bet with discipline instead of guesswork.

Open the Model See Pricing

Information only; not betting advice. Bet responsibly.

  • Promise: We de-vig market lines to estimate fair win probabilities, compute market-implied EV% versus the best available number, and translate that edge into Kelly-based stake suggestions sized to your bankroll and risk tolerance. You control the filters, Kelly factor, and books—so the model adapts to conservative or aggressive styles for disciplined, repeatable decisions.
  • Transparency: Every table shows when data was pulled, which books/prices were used, the implied probabilities, EV math, Kelly fraction, and the current model version—no black boxes.

About the Founder

About the Founder

Founder note

Hi, I’m Jalen Lee. I built Fair Value Sports to bring disciplined, transparent, value-driven betting to everyday bettors. With a background in finance and data analytics, I’m combining a love of sports with the rigor of modeling and visualization to help people focus on fair value, not hype.

What we stand for

Fair Value Sports doesn’t promise profits. It promises clarity: we surface the market’s fair value and your potential edge so every price, probability, and matchup is presented honestly and comparably. Sportsbooks build a margin (vig) into lines, giving the house an edge over time. We remove that vig, estimate fair win probabilities, and let you compare prices across books on equal footing and compute EV% against the best available price.

No “locks.” Just process. — Jalen
